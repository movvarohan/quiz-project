Instructions

Hope you are excited to play this fun quiz application!! Get ready to learn and test your knowledge!

To begin with you will have 15+ categories to choose from in which you will receive a 5 question quick quiz and will be able to either choose the category of easy or hard, which will indicate the strength of the questions in the selected category.

You will have 15 seconds for each question and if you answer within the 15 seconds and get it correct then you receive 2 points, if you answer after 15 seconds and get it correct then you receive 1 point. If you answer incorrectly there is no penalty and you just receive 0 points and will be given the correct answer.

If you want to come back to this page feel free to press the home button on the top right. Right next to the home button you should see MyScores and login buttons where if you create an account and login then you will be able to record your score and see improvement compared to past progress and can be a fun way to compete against friends, if you would like to play and compete for higher scores you will have a record of them.

Thanks for playing the Rohan Quiz App!

Government

The Constitution is the framework for the federal government of the United States of America. Written in 1787 and adopted in 1788, it carves the federal government into three branches. Generally, the legislative branch makes America's laws, the executive branch enforces the laws, and the judicial branch decides cases under the laws. The Constitution also gives the three branches duties outside the realm of the nation's laws. Easily learn with a simple to read constitution here
